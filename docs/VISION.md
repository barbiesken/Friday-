# FRIDAY — Product Vision

> **Your world. Orchestrated.**

FRIDAY is a personal desktop AI assistant that feels like an intelligent digital
being living inside your machine. Not a chatbot in a window — a **presence**.

## The feeling we are building

> "I don't open apps anymore. I talk to my computer."

Four words define every decision:

| Principle  | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| **Present**  | Always there, never in the way. A glance confirms it's alive. |
| **Aware**    | Knows the time, your context, your rhythm — anticipates. |
| **Calm**     | Never noisy, never nags. Invisible intelligence by default. |
| **Capable**  | Speaks, acts, controls the system, remembers across days. |

## The litmus test

Every feature must answer one question:

> **Does this feel like a premium operating system — or a gimmick?**

Only premium ships.

## The three interfaces

1. **Voice** (primary) — double-clap / "Friday" wake word / hotkey.
2. **Chat** (secondary) — for precision and quiet environments.
3. **Visual Presence** (always) — the Sphere.

The user should rarely type.

## The Sphere — the heart of FRIDAY

The entire product revolves around a single living object. Its behavior *is* the
product's emotional language. State is communicated visually before a word is spoken.

| State          | Visual language |
|----------------|-----------------|
| Idle           | Slow breathing, floating, internal stars |
| Listening      | Audio-reactive surface ripples |
| Thinking       | Internal particle storm |
| Speaking       | Pulsing energy synced to voice |
| Planning       | Orbiting satellites |
| Alert          | Red pulse |
| Sleep          | Dim blue, near-still |
| Celebrating    | Brief expansion, brighter glow |

## What FRIDAY is **not**

- Not a flat dashboard.
- Not a corporate UI.
- Not a generic chat app.
- Not a system that pretends to have feelings.
- Not something that speaks too much, greets repeatedly, or acts without permission.

## North-star experience

A 4-second cinematic boot. A sphere that breathes. A voice that arrives before
the screen fully resolves: *"Good morning, Aaryan."* Today's shape — energy,
meetings, the one priority — assembled around the orb. Then silence, until needed.
